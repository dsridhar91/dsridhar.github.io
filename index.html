Biography

I’m a postdoctoral researcher in the Data Science Institute at Columbia University, working with David Blei. My work focuses on applied causality. Broadly, I adapt modern ML and NLP methods for causal inference, especially from text data. I focus on applications to social science and algorithmic fairness.

I received my PhD from University of California Santa Cruz, where I worked with Lise Getoor.
